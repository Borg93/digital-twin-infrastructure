Highway in southern Norway

Norconsult delivers a multidisciplinary planning and design approach to clients in the Nordic region and beyond. Software and web development is handled by the daughter company Norconsult Informasjonssystemer (NoIS). Norwegian road authorities (Nye Veier) recently chose constructor AF Gruppen together with Norconsult to lead construction, design, and engineering on a new road linking Mandal and Kristiansand in southern Norway. This $500-million project will feature multiple tunnels and bridges throughout its 19-kilometer length, with one tunnel stretching more than 2.5 kilometers.
